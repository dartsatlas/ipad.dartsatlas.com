# Darts Atlas for iOS 9, 10, and 11

Published 16-Feb 2022


## Step 1: Passcode

Your iPad must have a passcode set and enabled. Disabling this at any point will prevent Darts Atlas from working, and you will need to repeat this process.


## Step 2: Install Updated Trust Certificate

You will be redirected to your iPad's Security Settings and prompted to install the certificate.

[Tap here to install](https://letsencrypt.org/certs/isrgrootx1.pem)


## Step 3: 

Confirm by going to [https://kiosk.dartsatlas.com](https://kiosk.dartsatlas.com) on your iPad. If the page loads without a security warning then the process is complete and you are now able to use the Darts Atlas Kiosk on your iPad.
